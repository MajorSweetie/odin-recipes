Pepper Steak

Savor the flavor with this delicious oven-baked Pepper Steak. Mom's way!

Inside this dish we have a pan-seared cube steak. Cube Steak really does the job without breaking the bank. Worcestershire sauce of your choice infused with Red and Green Bell Peppers, onions and garlic. A side of egg noodles, mashed potatoes, or rice with this dish is recommended- your guests will want seconds..

(Serves four)

Prep time: 15 mins

Cook time: 1 Hour 30 mins

Total time: 1 hour 45 mins

*Ingredients*

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon onion powder, or to taste
  • 1 pound cube steak
  • seasoned salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, or to taste
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (Optional)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

*Steps*

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine flour, garlic powder, and onion powder in a shallow bowl.
  3. Season steak with seasoned salt and pepper, then dredge in flour mixture; shake off any excess.
  4. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Sear steaks in hot oil, then transfer to a large casserole dish.
  5. Pour beef broth and Worcestershire sauce into the dish; cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake for one hour.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle onion, pepper, mushrooms, and garlic over top.
  8. Place back in oven andontinue to cook until steak is tender and vegetables are soft (about 20 to 30 minutes more).
